Comments Sentiment

YoutubeJam allows users and creators to recieve a sentiment score that provides a way to see the feedback on a Youtube Video without having to sit through a whole lot of coments. Users can see the sentiment score of each comment as well as the overall average of all the coments.

Channel Sentiment

Log in to see how your channel is doing without having to sit through an inordinate amount of comments. Just set your channel in the profile and we'll show you your sentiments from a bunch of your videos.

Autosave History

Returning User? No worries, all your history has been saved so that you can view the videos you have already seen.
